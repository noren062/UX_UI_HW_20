Augmented Reality Design Challenge

This prototype comes from an AR exercise I worked on to develop a way to save the contents of your fridge to your mobile device. By extension, users will never have to worry about buying eggs twice again. The app scans the content of a refrigerator and proposes recipes based solely on what you've got on hand.
